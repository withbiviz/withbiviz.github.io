About Us

WithBi Solutions Inc. is a full-service professional consulting organization focusing on business intelligence and reporting solutions.We specialize in providing flexible and affordable BI/data warehouse/ETL solutions to various industries, such as banks, insurance companies, Government,oil/gas companies,education institutions and so on.

For the past few years, we have continued to leverage our core competencies in reporting, BI implementation, training and on-going support of BI solutions to serve our clients in Alberta and British Columbia. Our consultants experienced with JAVA, Oracle Applications,SAS, Informatica, SQL Server, Tableau, COGNOS related products give you the confidence for a low risk, timely and cost effective implementation. We have the proven product knowledge and depth of experience needed to resolve issues and get our customers to a production status quickly.
