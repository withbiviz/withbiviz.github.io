Our Services

Our consultants provide BI solutions to allow companies easily turn the data they collected and stored into meaningful information to best manage their operations. When key information is readily accessible, you can make better business decisions.

We also provide data analytics and reporting solutions to allow individuals throughout a company to assess business performance from a number of angles, based on data from numerous sources. A shared, consolidated view of key business performance measurements is crucial in supporting decision-making. Our solution features query, reporting, on-line analytical processing (OLAP), data mining and performance measurement technology to improve the analysis of business processes and customer information.

Besides information we extract from customer's raw data, we also provide services to help customer gain knowledge such as data visualizatioin, statistics modeling or data mining. If the volumn of raw data is huge and unstructured, we also provide big data processing service before our analysis services.
